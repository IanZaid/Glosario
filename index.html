Vocabulary Glossary: Unit 9

Contrast and Addition Connectors

Word Phonetics (IPA) Translation Example
Although/ɔːlˈðoʊ/Aunque
Although it is cold, I am happy.
However/haʊˈev.ər/Sin embargo
I like pizza. However, I am full.
Furthermore/ˌfɜːr.ðəˈmɔːr/Además
It is fast. Furthermore, it is cheap.
Instead/ɪnˈsted/En su lugar
Don't run. Instead, walk slowly.
Otherwise/ˈʌð.ə.waɪz/De lo contrario
Eat now; otherwise, you'll be hungry.
Despite/dɪˈspaɪt/A pesar de
I ran despite the rain.
Moreover/mɔːrˈoʊ.vər/Por otra parte
She is smart. Moreover, she is kind.
Yet/jet/Aun así
It is small, yet very strong.
Nonetheless/ˌnʌn.ðəˈles/No obstante
It was hard; nonetheless, I finished.
In addition/ɪn əˈdɪʃ.ən/Adicionalmente
In addition to milk, I need bread.

Vocabulary Glossary: Unit 10

Cause, Effect and Precise Language

Word Phonetics (IPA) Translation Example
Therefore/ˈðer.fɔːr/Por lo tanto
I studied; therefore, I passed.
Since/sɪns/Ya que
Since you are here, let's play.
Thus/ðʌs/Así
He fell, thus he is crying.
So that/soʊ ðæt/Para que
I study so that I can learn.
Unless/ʌnˈles/A menos que
I won't go unless you go.
Actually/ˈæk.tʃu.ə.li/De hecho
Actually, I prefer the blue car.
Regarding/rɪˈɡɑːr.dɪŋ/Respecto a
Regarding the game, I am ready.
Overall/ˌoʊ.vərˈɔːl/En general
Overall, it was a good day.
Such as/sʌtʃ æz/Tales como
I like pets, such as cats and dogs.
While/waɪl/Mientras que
I like tea, while he likes juice.
